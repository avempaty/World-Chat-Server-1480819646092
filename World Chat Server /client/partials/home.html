Our Vision
With this beautifully designed web application, you'll be able to communicate to your
community with ease. ChatBox started with the simple idea of connecting people within
communities. With Chatbox, you will be able to find new people, groups, activities,
and more. This can be achieved with just a message into the chat.
We are excited to deliever this to you with an elegantly crafted
chat design to make your experience better and more personal. We want you to
focus on getting to know your community.

We are a close team of dedicated developers based out of Purdue University. We are passionate
about using cutting-edge technology and developing innovative video games.
